The Story of a King

LeBron James was born on December 30, 1984, in Akron, Ohio. Raised by a single mother, LeBron found purpose through basketball. His games at St. Vincent-St. Mary High School were broadcast on ESPN, and Sports Illustrated put him on the cover as a 17-year-old with the headline "The Chosen One."

Why LeBron is the GOAT

  • He has won 4 NBA Championships with 3 different teams
  • He is the all-time NBA scoring leader with 43,000+ points
  • He has never had a losing season in 23 years
  • His top accomplishments prove he is untouchable:
    1. 4x NBA Champion
    2. 4x Finals MVP
    3. 4x League MVP
    4. 22x NBA All-Star
    5. 2024 Olympics MVP
    6. 2025 Hall of Fame Inductee
  • He delivered Cleveland its first championship in 52 years
  • He opened a school for at-risk kids in his hometown of Akron
